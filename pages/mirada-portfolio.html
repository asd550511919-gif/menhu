Mirada Travel 是一个只提供「在地体验旅行」的高端旅行品牌。
我们认为马塞尔—普鲁斯特提到的"真正的发现之旅不在于寻找新的景观，而在于拥有新的眼光。"就是旅行产品设计的本质。如果没有新鲜的视角和世界观，那行程只是枯燥无味的景点拼凑。我们希望呈现给不同客户的是我们理解的、不同视角下的欧洲，这才是产品而非景点的拼凑。

Mirada Travel is a high-end travel brand dedicated exclusively to immersive local-experience journeys.We believe that what Marcel Proust once expressed — "The real voyage of discovery consists not in seeking new landscapes, but in having new eyes." — captures the true essence of travel product design. Without a fresh perspective or worldview, an itinerary is nothing more than a mechanical collection of sights.What we aim to present to each client is Europe as we understand it through different lenses and perspectives. That is what transforms travel into a thoughtfully crafted product — rather than a simple compilation of destinations.
